How It Works

A Straightforward Process. An Honest Assessment.

We don't make this more complicated than it needs to be. Here's what working with Abria actually looks like from first contact to funded.

1

Initial Consultation — No Obligation

You tell us about your business: what you need, how much, what you've already tried, and where things stand financially. We ask direct questions and give you an equally direct read on what's realistic.

At this stage, no commitment is required from either side. The goal is to understand your situation well enough to tell you honestly whether we can help — and if so, what that looks like.

2

File Assessment

We review your current financial position, credit profile, business structure, and any existing documentation. We identify what's working in your favour and — more importantly — what's likely to work against you with a lender.

If your file needs work, we tell you exactly what needs to change and why. This step alone has saved clients significant time and prevented declined applications they didn't need to experience.

3

Deal Packaging — Where We Do the Work

This is the stage that separates Abria from a standard broker. We prepare your complete lender-ready file: business plan (if needed), financial summaries, use-of-funds narrative, executive summary, and supporting documentation.

We build the strongest possible version of your application before a single lender sees it. The file goes in complete, credible, and structured to answer the questions a lender will ask before they ask them.

4

Lender Matching & Submission

We identify the right financing channel based on your profile — bank, credit union, alternative lender, private lender, or investor. Different businesses suit different lender types, and sending the wrong application to the wrong lender is a fast route to a decline that damages your next attempt.

We present your file to sources most likely to engage at the terms your situation supports — and we manage the communication from submission through to decision.

5

Close & Fund

We stay with you through the final stages — term review, condition management, and funding confirmation. We don't disappear when the hard part starts.

Once you're funded, we follow up to ensure the arrangement is performing as expected and that any future financing needs are planned for properly.

What We Don't Do

We don't guarantee approvals. No reputable broker can, and anyone who tells you otherwise is misleading you. We don't submit incomplete applications and hope for the best. We don't disappear once a submission is made.

If we don't think we can help you — we'll tell you in the first conversation. That's more useful than weeks of false optimism.
