About Abria

A Capital Advisory Firm Built on Operator Experience

We started Abria because we kept seeing the same problem — strong businesses failing to access capital because their file was wrong, not their business.

Who we are

We've Been on Both Sides of This Table

Abria Capital is a business funding and capital advisory firm. We work with Canadian business owners who need financing — helping them assess their position, prepare a lender-ready file, and connect with the right capital source for their situation.

Our principals have operated businesses, dealt with lenders, and navigated the Canadian capital market from the inside. That experience shapes every assessment we do, every file we build, and every lender conversation we have on a client's behalf.

We're not a bank. We're not a software platform that scores your application and routes you somewhere anonymous. We're a firm — with real people who understand what's on both sides of a lending decision.

What makes Abria different

We build the file before we send it. Most capital brokers are essentially referral services — they take your information and send it to lenders. We prepare, package, and strengthen the application before any lender sees it. The file quality often determines the outcome.

We're direct about what's realistic. If we don't think we can help you, we'll tell you in the first conversation. That's more useful than false optimism and weeks of wasted time.

We stay through to the end. We don't disappear after a submission. We manage the lender relationship, handle conditions, and support the close.

Our approach

Capital Advisory, Not Paper Shuffling

The capital brokerage space in Canada has a problem: most brokers are paid to move applications, not to improve them. The result is that business owners with solid operations get declined — not because they don't qualify, but because their file didn't make the case.

Abria was built around a different model. We assess before we submit. We prepare before we present. We tell you what's working in your favour and what's working against you — then we fix the second category before a lender ever sees the file.

This approach takes more work on our end. It also produces better outcomes.
