Automotive & Dealership Advisory

Operational and strategic consulting for Canadian automotive dealers, dealer groups, and automotive professionals — from people with deep roots in the industry.

The automotive retail business is unlike most other industries. The margin dynamics, the financing model, the relationship between new and used, and the complexity of the F&I office require a specific kind of understanding. Our automotive advisory team brings that understanding from direct experience — not from the outside looking in.

Where We Add Value

  • Sales Process Optimization From the first contact to the delivery — reviewing and improving the end-to-end sales process, identifying where deals are being lost, and building a consistent customer experience that drives both volume and gross.
  • F&I Performance Advisory Finance and insurance is often the highest-margin department in the dealership — and the most underdeveloped. We advise on product mix, penetration rates, menu presentation, and compliance.
  • Team Structure & Management Recruiting, compensation structures, performance management, and retention in the automotive context — building a team that performs consistently rather than one that relies on a few top producers.
  • Dealership Operations Operational efficiency, process design, interdepartmental coordination, and the management systems that keep a high-volume retail operation running smoothly.
  • Business Strategy & Growth Strategic planning for dealer groups looking to expand, acquire, or improve their competitive position in their market.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions About Automotive Advisory

How do I improve F&I performance at my dealership?+

F&I performance improvement typically comes from a combination of better product mix, improved menu presentation, higher penetration rates, and a consistent process. Our team has direct F&I experience across high-volume dealerships and can assess exactly where your performance gaps are and what changes will move the metrics fastest.

How can a dealership improve its sales process?+

A strong dealership sales process is consistent, customer-focused, and managed actively at every stage — from first contact through delivery. Common gaps include inconsistent follow-up, poor CRM usage, and a lack of structured coaching for the sales team. We assess your current process and build practical improvements that drive both volume and gross.

What does an automotive dealership consultant do?+

An automotive dealership consultant assesses operations, identifies performance gaps, and provides specific recommendations to improve profitability, sales volume, and customer experience. Abria's automotive advisory team has direct operating experience in dealership environments — we have been inside the sales tower, the F&I office, and the management meetings. That context makes the advice specific and actionable.

How do I recruit better automotive sales staff in Ontario?+

Recruiting for automotive retail requires a clear compensation structure, a compelling value proposition for candidates, and a process that identifies coachable, motivated people rather than just experienced ones. We advise on the full recruiting cycle — from job posting to onboarding — and have a network of automotive professionals across Ontario.
