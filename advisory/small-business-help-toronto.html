Whether you are fighting cash flow problems, dealing with a difficult partner, losing ground to competition, or simply feeling stuck — experienced outside perspective changes the outcome.

Our Approach

We Know What This Feels Like

You built something. You have put years, money, and yourself into it. And right now it is not working the way it should — or it is not working at all. You are not sure if the problem is fixable. You are not sure who to call. And you are not sure you can afford to bring in expensive consultants when cash is already tight.

We do not send junior analysts. We do not write reports nobody reads. We sit across the table from you, understand your specific situation, and tell you honestly what we see and what we think you should do.

Sometimes the answer is a clear operational fix. Sometimes it is a structural reset. Sometimes it is an honest conversation about whether the business is viable — and what the best exit looks like. Whatever the situation, you deserve a clear-eyed outside view from someone who has been there.

Where We Help

Situations We Help With

Cash Flow Problems

Revenue is coming in but the bank account stays thin. We help identify where money is leaking, how to manage working capital, and how to build financial systems that keep you solvent through slow seasons.

Growth Plateau

Revenue has stalled and you cannot figure out why. We look at your model, your market, your operations, and your team — and identify the real barriers to the next level.

Partner or Shareholder Disputes

A broken partnership can destroy a healthy business. We help owners understand their options — from restructuring the relationship to facilitating a buyout — before legal costs consume the equity built.

CRA or Compliance Pressure

A tax problem or regulatory issue can feel like a crisis even when it is manageable. We help you understand what you are facing, organize your position, and engage the right professionals effectively.

Operational Breakdown

Systems, staff, process, or leadership — when the day-to-day stops working, growth becomes impossible. We identify the operational root causes and build a practical plan to address them.

Considering Closing or Selling

Sometimes the right answer is an exit — and the best exits are planned, not panicked. We help you understand the value of what you have built and how to maximize it on the way out.
