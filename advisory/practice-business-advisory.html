Practice Area 01

Business Advisory & Strategy

Senior advisory for Canadian business owners, entrepreneurs, and executives — at every stage of the business lifecycle.

Most business owners are too close to their business to see it clearly. The decisions that matter most — when to scale, when to restructure, when to sell, how to compete — benefit enormously from an experienced outside perspective. That is what our business advisory practice provides.

What We Do

Our advisory team brings over 26 years of direct operating experience — building, managing, and scaling businesses across multiple industries in Canada. We have been through the decisions our clients face. That firsthand experience is what distinguishes our guidance from theoretical consulting.

We work with clients on the full range of business strategy challenges — from day-to-day operational decisions to multi-year growth plans, acquisitions, and exit strategies. We also prepare comprehensive business plans for clients across all industries, whether for internal direction, investor presentations, or financing applications.

  • Strategic PlanningVision, goals, roadmap, and execution framework. We facilitate planning processes that produce strategies teams can actually execute against.

  • Business Plan DevelopmentComprehensive, professionally prepared business plans for any purpose — financing, investment, partnership, or internal direction.

Who We Work With

Our business advisory clients are typically owner-operators of established businesses facing a specific inflection point — a growth plateau, a potential acquisition, a succession question, or a competitive threat that requires a fresh strategic perspective. We also work with earlier-stage entrepreneurs who want to build on a solid strategic foundation from the start.

Abria Advisory is headquartered in the Greater Toronto Area and works with clients across Canada and internationally. Our engagements are not constrained by geography — we bring the same standard of advisory to clients in every market, whether the work is conducted in person, remotely, or both.

Common Questions About Business Advisory

What is a business advisory firm?+

A business advisory firm provides strategic guidance, operational expertise, and experienced outside perspective to business owners and executives. Unlike consulting firms that deliver reports, advisory firms work alongside clients over time — helping them make better decisions, navigate challenges, and execute on their growth plans. Abria Advisory is a boutique management consulting and advisory firm headquartered in the Greater Toronto Area.

What is the difference between a business advisor and a consultant?+

Consultants typically deliver a defined project with a report or recommendation at the end. Advisors work alongside you over time, staying involved in implementation, adjusting as circumstances change, and building a genuine understanding of your business. Many clients need both at different stages — Abria offers both models depending on what the situation requires.

At Abria, we offer hourly advisory and monthly retainer arrangements specifically designed for small and mid-sized businesses. Our fees are structured to reflect the reality of a small business — not the rates of a large consulting firm. The initial consultation is always complimentary, and we are transparent about costs before any engagement begins.
