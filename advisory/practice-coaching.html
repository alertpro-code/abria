Business Coaching for Entrepreneurs & Professionals

Direct, substantive business coaching for entrepreneurs, independent professionals, and commission-based practitioners across Canada. Not a program — genuine guidance from advisors who have owned their outcome.

Running your own business or working on commission is one of the most rewarding and most isolating things a person can do. The decisions are entirely yours. So is the weight of them. Our business coaching practice exists to change that — to give you an experienced, candid advisor who understands the reality of self-employment and brings perspective that only comes from having lived it.

Who This Is For

This practice is built for professionals who work for themselves — people for whom the decisions are entirely their own, and for whom the consequences of those decisions are personal and immediate. That tends to include entrepreneurs at every stage, independent practitioners in financial services and professional fields, commission-based professionals building their practice, and anyone navigating a significant career or business transition.

What they share is an absence of the structures that employed professionals take for granted — no board to answer to, no manager to escalate to, no institutional support when a hard decision needs to be made. Our coaching practice exists to fill that gap with something more valuable: experienced, direct, and genuinely independent perspective.

What Makes This Different

This is not a program. There are no modules, no worksheets, and no generic frameworks applied regardless of context. What we offer is direct, substantive engagement from advisors who have built and operated businesses across multiple industries — people who understand what it means to own the outcome because they have done exactly that.

The coaching relationship at Abria is built around the individual. Each engagement begins with a clear-eyed assessment of where you are, where you are trying to go, and what is genuinely in the way. What follows is honest, experienced guidance — the kind that is only possible when the advisor has no stake in telling you what you want to hear.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions About Business Coaching

What does a business coach actually do?+

A business coach provides experienced outside perspective on your business decisions, holds you accountable to your goals, and helps you identify what is actually in the way of your growth. At Abria, our coaches are practitioners who have built and operated businesses themselves — so the guidance is direct and experience-based, not theoretical.

How much does business coaching cost in Ontario?+

Business coaching fees vary widely. At Abria, we offer flexible engagement models including hourly advisory and monthly retainer arrangements designed to be accessible for self-employed professionals and small business owners. Our monthly retainer model provides ongoing advisory access at a cost most clients recoup in a single improved business outcome. The initial consultation is always complimentary.

Who needs a business coach?+

Anyone who works for themselves and wants to earn more, grow faster, or make better decisions benefits from business coaching. Self-employed professionals — real estate agents, insurance and mortgage brokers, financial advisors, contractors, and entrepreneurs — consistently benefit because they lack the internal structure that employed professionals take for granted.

Can a business coach help me make more money?+

Yes — specifically when the coaching focuses on revenue model, pricing, sales process, and the specific behaviours and decisions that drive income. Our coaching practice is built around practical income growth: identifying where money is being left on the table and building a clear plan to capture it.

Where can I find a business coach in Toronto or the GTA?+

Abria Advisory is headquartered in the Greater Toronto Area and works with clients across Ontario and Canada. We conduct sessions in person, by phone, or by video — so geography is never a constraint. The initial discovery call is complimentary and can be booked directly through our contact page.
