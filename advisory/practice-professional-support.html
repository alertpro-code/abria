Professional & Regulatory Support

Experienced guidance on legal, accounting, and regulatory matters — from professionals who have navigated these areas at the highest levels, at a fraction of large-firm rates.

Large law firms and accounting practices provide essential services — but their rates often put meaningful professional guidance out of reach for small and mid-sized businesses. Our professional support practice bridges that gap, providing experienced, practical guidance on legal, tax, and regulatory matters at accessible rates.

Important note: Abria Advisory is not a law firm or accounting firm, and our team members in this practice do not provide formal legal or accounting services. What we offer is experienced, practical guidance from professionals with deep backgrounds in these fields — guidance that helps clients make better decisions and ask better questions of the licensed professionals they retain.

What We Provide

  • Contract Review & Interpretation Plain-language review of business contracts, agreements, and documents. We help clients understand what they are signing before the lawyers bill for it.
  • Business Tax Planning Guidance Practical context on Canadian business and personal tax as it relates to entrepreneurs, self-employed professionals, and business owners — to inform decisions and ensure the right questions are asked of your accountant.
  • Corporate Structure Advisory Guidance on business structure, incorporation considerations, and the organizational frameworks that affect tax, liability, and growth — from experienced operators who have structured businesses themselves.
  • Regulatory Navigation Understanding the regulatory landscape for your industry — licensing requirements, compliance obligations, and how to work within them effectively.
  • Financial Statement Review A business-owner's perspective on your financial statements — what the numbers mean, what they suggest about the health of the business, and where the risks and opportunities lie.
