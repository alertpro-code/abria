Durham Region Business Financing — What We See

Durham Region's business community spans construction and trades, transportation and logistics, food service, healthcare, retail, and professional services. The financing needs are real and consistent — equipment purchases, working capital gaps, expansion capital, and in many cases, help recovering from a bank decline.

What we've found working with Durham Region businesses is that the challenge is rarely the business itself. It's the application. Local businesses with solid revenue and genuine financing needs get declined regularly because the documentation is incomplete, the use-of-funds narrative is vague, or they approached the wrong lender for their profile.
